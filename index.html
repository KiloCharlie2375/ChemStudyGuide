Fundamentals

Nomenclature, ions, bonding, Lewis structures, oxidation rules.

  • Ionic: NaCl → Sodium chloride
  • Variable-charge: FeCl2 → Iron(II) chloride
  • Covalent: CO2 → Carbon dioxide
  • Acids: HCl → Hydrochloric acid; H2SO4 → Sulfuric acid
  • NH4+ — Ammonium
  • NO3− — Nitrate
  • SO42− — Sulfate
  • CO32− — Carbonate
  • OH− — Hydroxide
  • C2H3O2− — Acetate
  • Ionic — electron transfer
  • Covalent — electron sharing
  • Metallic — electron sea
  1. Count valence e⁻
  2. Sketch skeleton
  3. Add lone pairs & bonds
  • Element = 0
  • Group 1 = +1
  • O = −2 (except −1 in peroxides)
